Becoming a CNSSO means committing to the invisible infrastructure that keeps every flight safe. My aspiration is to master the full CNS spectrum. Not just maintaining systems, but understanding them deeply enough to anticipate failures before they occur. I aim to carry that standard by building a culture of proactive maintenance, technical excellence, and knowledge-sharing, making every airspace I serve more predictable, safe, and resilient. This CNSSO journey also thought me that no matter how well prepared you are, there are things that we can’t control and that go out of our way. You just have to be resilient and adapt to every challenge and hardship that you’ve faced and enjoy the process.
