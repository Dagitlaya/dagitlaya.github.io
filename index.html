About Us

DAGITAB (electric spark) + LAYA (freedom)

As future CNSSOs, electricity represents the core of our discipline: powering the systems of communication, navigation, and surveillance that ensure safe and efficient air traffic management. With members from electrical, electronics, and mechanical engineering backgrounds, "𝐃𝐚𝐠𝐢𝐭𝐚𝐛" symbolizes the spark of innovation, precision, and excellence that unites us. Dagitlaya 45 embodies the spark that enables the freedom of flight. We are the unseen force behind every signal, navigation aid, and communication link that keeps airspace safe, structured, and continuously connected. Like electricity, we are precise, reliable, and essential. In this context, "𝐋𝐚𝐲𝐚" (freedom) represents the ability of aviation to operate seamlessly within a highly organized system. It reflects the confidence of flight, made possible through accurate navigation, reliable surveillance, and uninterrupted communication. It is freedom defined by safety, coordination, and precision.
